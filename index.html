About Me

Hello! I’m Ralph Reijs. CTO and Co-Founder at Mpowa, and advisor to various other blockchain startups. I solve complex challenges and provide solutions in infrastructure, software architecture and security.

I am a cryptocurrency / blockchain specialist with more than 22 years’ experience at the cutting edge of IT with a background in digital infrastructure / infotech security. I have been a computer enthusiast since the age of 10, vividly remembering the BBS days, blueboxing, 2600 and Phrack Magazine, and have since built an extensive expertise in hardware and software.

Having been a crypto-enthusiast since a young age, and passionate about the disruptive potential of Bitcoin and blockchain since 2009, I started mining and developed an iOS Top 10 Finance app 'iBitcoin' in 2010 (until I integrated MtGox and Apple killed it).

The skillset I've developed has led me to be Head of IT Security at Pillar, one of the first ICOs in 2017: I was part of the core team during ICO, helped raise $27M and led the Infrastructure / Security team for 2 years. In 2018, as part of the Jinbi Token Sale, I helped to raise $47.5M and took role as CTO.

My integrity, creativity and competence in blockchain-related topics has resulted in clients requesting bespoke products, including design and development of an automated ETH + ERC20 tumbling/mixing platform. In 2019 I co-founded Mpowa, dedicated to building a Decentralised Autonomous Platform around Tokenization of zero-point energy technologies.

Prior to this, I was an ITSM Solution Architect for more than 40 clients, including Shell, Rabobank, Bayer and Deutsche Bank, leading developers and process owners, mediating at the management level between internal teams. This included long periods in Europe and the Middle East.

I have exceptional analytical and creative skills: I have been writing code and creating content in many languages, frameworks and application suites, as well as designing beautiful, intuitive interfaces and am always looking for ways to transform ideas into commercial or educational products: from mobile apps to embedded-computing based prototypes.

I'm deeply passionate about decentralised, empowering innovation and its role in effecting positive change in our society.
